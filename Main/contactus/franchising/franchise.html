Savoury Sandwich Franchise Opportunity


Welcome to Savoury Sandwich, where we are excited to offer you the opportunity to join our successful franchise family. As a renowned brand in the sandwich industry, we invite passionate entrepreneurs to be a part of our growth story.


Why Choose Savoury Sandwich Franchise:

  1. Proven Business Model: Benefit from our established and successful business model.
  2. Brand Recognition: Leverage our strong brand presence and customer loyalty.
  3. Comprehensive Support: Receive extensive training, marketing assistance, and ongoing operational support.
  4. Unique Brand Values: Join a franchise that is guided by a set of core values that make us stand out in the market.

What Sets Us Apart:

  • Artisanal Ingredients: We source the finest, locally-sourced ingredients to create a unique and flavorful experience.
  • Customization Galore: Our menu offers a wide range of options, but we take it a step further by allowing customers to customize their sandwiches to their liking.
  • Signature Sauces: Our exclusive house-made sauces are crafted to perfection, adding a special touch to every bite.
  • Community Engagement: We actively participate in community events and initiatives, fostering a sense of connection and giving back to our loyal customers.

Franchisee Requirements:

Financial Qualifications: Minimum liquid capital of RM 50,000.
Experience: Previous experience in the food industry is preferred but not mandatory. We provide comprehensive training to all franchisees.


Investment Details:

Initial Franchise Fee: RM 30,000
Equipment Costs: Estimated at RM 50,000 (Includes kitchen equipment, signage, and initial inventory)
Ongoing Royalties: 5% of monthly gross sales


Support and Training:

Comprehensive Training: Receive in-depth training covering all aspects of our operations.
Operational Support: Benefit from ongoing assistance in areas such as marketing, supply chain, and store setup.
