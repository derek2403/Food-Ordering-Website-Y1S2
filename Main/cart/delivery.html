    Delivery Policies

    • We currently offer delivery within Klang Valley. To check if we deliver to your location, please enter your address at the checkout.
    • If the delivery time/ pick-up time is left empty, we will assume that you want us to prepare as soon as possible.
    • A flat delivery fee of RM10 will be applied to all orders. This fee helps cover the cost of our delivery service and ensures a reliable and timely delivery experience for our customers.
    • Upon successful placement of your order, you will receive an email or SMS confirmation with the details of your purchase, including the estimated delivery time.
    • For your convenience, we offer order tracking. You will receive a link to track your delivery in real-time once your order is out for delivery.
    • If you have any questions or concerns regarding your delivery, please contact our customer support at 03-61248888. We are here to assist you.
